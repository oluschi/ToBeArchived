A Journey into the
Curation of Contemporary
Fashion Styling
About

To Be Archived explores the intersection of contemporary fashion styling through the digital and physical archives. Through an analysis of my personal archive of outfit styling collected over a 30 day period, this project will provide insights into the ways in which one could document their journey with fashion styling and telal a story through their fashion choices. This project will be of interest to those fascinated by the ways in which our past and present are captured through fashion and will provide a unique perspective on my journey with fashion styling.
